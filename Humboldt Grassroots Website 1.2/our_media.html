ABOUT RHIZOME:

From the folks who bring you the Humboldt Anarchist Book Fair, Humboldt Grassroots is stoked to present Rhizome Infoshop. Rhizome Infoshop is a multi-media radical lending library. After years of bouncing around from the Ink Annex to Synapsis Incubator, we have created a lending system where you send us a request to borrow an item and we meet in person to lend it to you. Our collection consists of books, zines, films and audio on anarchism, feminism, anti-capitalism, politics, queer & ethnic studies to ecology, DIY, history, literature, kids books, art and much much more!
